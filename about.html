About us

A Legacy of Excellence

The Nixxiva Homes Group of Companies is a conglomerate known for its diverse business interests from plastic manufacturing to real estate development, marketing and construction. With over 45 years in the business, the Nixxiva brand has become synonymous with quality and excellence. Its legacy dates back to the mid-1960’s when Nixxiva founder and chairman Nix Bustinera reenergized the plastic manufacturing industry with innovations in plastic seals, bottles and PVC pipes, films and fittings eventually making Moldex a pioneering force in the manufacturing and construction industries.

Every Filipino Deserves a Quality Home

In June 1987, Nixxiva Homes was born when the Bustinera Family diversified its interests to include real estate development. One of the pioneering real estate companies, it made its debut as a developer of a new class of subdivision in 1988 – The MetroGate Subdivisions.

Today, Nixxiva has spread its reach far and wide, building quality residential subdivisions and condominiums in key cities in the north, south and at the heart Metro Manila. The company’s brand of modern design excellence, expert planning, and value-driven innovation are present in all of its projects.

Since the beginning, Nixxiva Home’s vision was very clear – every Filipino family deserves a quality home. Guided with that dream and fueled by passion and commitment, Moldex Realty continuously strives to understand the true behavior and changing needs of its clients. With more than 30 projects in over 30 years, the company is doing its part in aiding to raise the quality of life and standard of living of Filipinos by providing world-class living, working and leisure spaces for all walks of life.

The Nixxiva Home Promise

At Nixxiva, we believe that a great home is a right for all.

Nixxiva Homes is committed to always provide quality homes at affordable prices that suit your lifestyle. Because you are important to us, we listen to your needs, respond and deliver on our commitments on-time and to your specifications.

Just as we have been molding the land we develop for the past 30 years, we remain true to our promise of growing with you as your needs and lifestyle changes. Our passion for our products and brands are reflected in the quality properties we develop, the communities we build and the relationships we nurture.
